3D Printing Lab

About the Project

Our 3D Printing Lab empowers students to bring their ideas to life, producing functional prototypes, custom parts, and artistic designs. From rocketry nosecones to CubeSat components, our lab is the heartbeat of our club's builds.

Objectives

  • Teach students 3D modeling software and slicing tools.
  • Produce project components for rocketry and space systems.
  • Encourage creative design and rapid prototyping.
