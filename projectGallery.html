PROJECT GALLERY

MakerSat

The MakerSat is our primary project.

It is our entry for the NASA TechRise/CSLI (CubeSat Launch Initiative) events.

Prometheus-Alpha Payload

Prometheus-Alpha is the the software and hardware aboard the MakerSat that will carry out our mission plan.

Our current mission plan is to gather information on particles in space that can tell us about solar events.

Prometheus-Sigma Propulsion System

Prometheus-Sigma is the primary propulsion system for the MakerSat.
