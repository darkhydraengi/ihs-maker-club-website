CubeSat Program

About the Project

Our CubeSat program is dedicated to launching Irvington High's first satellite into orbit. Students will design, build, and test a fully operational CubeSat capable of gathering scientific data from space.

Objectives

  • Design a 1U CubeSat frame and payload.
  • Collaborate with aerospace partners for launch opportunities.
  • Gather data on atmospheric conditions and solar radiation.
