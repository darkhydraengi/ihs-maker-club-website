Srihari Muralidharan



Hi! I’m Srihari Muralidharan, the president of Maker Club.

I’m passionate about hands-on engineering, electronics, and bringing creative ideas to life.

Whether it’s building CubeSats, designing custom drones, or tinkering with Arduino projects, I love exploring new ways to make technology accessible and exciting for everyone.

Through Maker Club, I hope to create a welcoming space where students can learn, collaborate, and turn their boldest ideas into real working projects.
